Our Story

I have been an animal lover my whole life. I couldn't imagine a world without animals.

Growing up, we were strictly a dog family. No. Cats. Allowed. In fact, I was fully under the impression that I despised cats.

Don't fret! I learned very quickly just how wrong a person could be!

It was when I rang in my 25th year that I decided to move out of the parents house, get a little place, and start being an "adult". How could this hapen without a pet, you ask?

IT CAN'T!

Meet The Babies!

I adopted my two kittens from Open Door Animal Sanctuary in September of 2017. It was such an easy process. They have a kitten room where the kittens are free roaming. It made it super easy to see each kittens personality and how they interacted with each other! It was also a good way to see not only which one you liked, but which one actually liked you!

Initially, I wanted to adopt one kitten. That did not end up being the case (thank goodness)! They recommended adopting two kittens as oposed to one, because that way they have a buddy to tire them out. If you adopt just one kitten, you run the risk of said kitten becoming nocturnal. NOT ideal for someone who loves sleep as much as I do! Enter: Lola and Oliver.
